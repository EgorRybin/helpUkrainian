Who we are?

WHO WE ARE? We are a Non-Governmental organization «Little Steps for Ukraine» based in Longford, Ireland. Our organization is quite small, however, we do a big job. We are trying to give Ukrainian children an opportunity to smile, to become a little bit happier. We are a group of volunteers who are sending boxes with gifts to children who are living in Ukraine, who are suffering from the invasion, and who don't have a normal childhood because of the War.

What are we sending?

We send presents to Ukrainian children. We send small boxes with useful and joyful things:

  • toys
  • clothes
  • books
  • accessories
  • pencils, drawing markers, coloring books
  • sweets
We send anything that makes kids happy.
